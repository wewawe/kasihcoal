Commitment

PT. Kasih Coal Resources is commited to improvements of standard of living in Indonesia. Through eduction, we believe human resources could excell in globally challenged world. We dedicate our support to STIE KASIH BANGSA , a full scholarship campus, to support the needs of higher education in Indonesia.

STIE Kasih Bangsa

Kasih Bangsa is a business school in Jakarta, Indonesia that has been accredited by Indonesian Government and having more than 300 graduates of bachelor of art since the school opened. We believe that investment in education has a strong connection to improving the living standards for future generations. We are helping and teaching our future generations to think critically and effectively through our academic program that specialized on both management and accounting programs.
